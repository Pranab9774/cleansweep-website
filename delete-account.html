Overview

Request deletion of data associated with your CleanSweep support or app account records.

  • Email supportcleansweepapp@gmail.com with the subject “CleanSweep Data Deletion Request”.
  • Include the email address used with the app or support request.
  • We may ask for verification before processing deletion.
  • Some records may be retained where required for security, legal or abuse-prevention reasons.

For help, email supportcleansweepapp@gmail.com.
