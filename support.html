Support

How can we help?

For app issues, feedback, Play Store questions or privacy requests, contact CleanSweep support.

Email supportcleansweepapp@gmail.com

Before contacting support

  • Share your device model and Android version.
  • Share the CleanSweep app version.
  • Describe what happened and when it happened.
  • Attach screenshots if they help explain the issue.

Common topics

Scanning, duplicate results, Deep AI Analysis, custom folder access, app feedback and Play Store support.
